Goa


Goa is a famous place in India and many people visit there in the summer season.

Top 5 Attractions in Goa

  1. Fish Camping - It provides an amazing experience.
  2. Scuba Diving - This is also a very amazing experience.
  3. Telugu Pub - Telugu Pub is amazing and many visitors frequent there.
  4. Parachute - Parachute is a must visit place.
  5. Beach - The beach is a major attraction.

Must Try Foods in Goa

  • Fish Fry - It is a very tasty food.
  • Sweets - These sweets are available only in Goa.
  • Hot Chicken - It is a very tasty food.
  • Wine - Available at a cheap price.
  • Fried Rice - Fried Rice is a tasty food.
